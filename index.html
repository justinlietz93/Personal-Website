About Me

I am a motivated and self-driven software development student currently maintaining a perfect 4.0 GPA at Fox Valley Technical College. My enthusiasm lies in creating and problem-solving my way through software projects. I have a solid foundation established through coursework and personal projects.

I have good proficiency across a spectrum of programming languages and frameworks, including C#, C++, Python, PHP, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and SQL/TSQL. I am determined and excited for continuous learning and refining my expertise, consistently seeking opportunities to broaden my knowledge and enhance my skill set.

Why I love software development:

Ever since I used a computer to learn the alphabet when I was in preschool, I have felt like software was magic. I always wondered how it worked, and what was going on inside the machine. Now, my curiosity has been fueled continually by the evolving world of technology.

What I'm passionate about:

I'm really inspired by the complexity and potency of software and how specialized individuals form teams to craft intuitive, user-friendly software. It's fascinating to be part of the magic behind the scenes.

What I'm currently working on:

I am currently working on a personal project that will be an iOS-based application that will allow users to create and manage their own workout routines using AI. I have used many workout apps in the past and I see a lot of room for improvement. I am excited to see where this project takes me.
